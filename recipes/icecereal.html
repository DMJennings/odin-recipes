Ice Cereal

Description

This is one of the most cooling breakfasts' available! Now when there is truly nothing to eat you can easily give your guests this hydrating meal that can be made for pennies on the dollar!

Ingredients

  • A cereal bowl of your choosing
  • Any spoon
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Get our your favorite cereal bowl and spoon combo!
  2. Add any ice you have on hand into the bowl.
  3. Thats it! Now its time to enjoy this truly hydrating, zero calorie breakfast!
