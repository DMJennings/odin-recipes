Everest's Gourmet Meal

Description

This is a fan favoirte of man's best friend. A delicious mixture of kibble, chicken stew, and who could forget the aromatic wild fish oil as a topping.

While most might find this particular dish to be a travesty in the kitchen, your canine friends who so patiently wait for just a mere morsel of your dinner would be more than happy to be face first in their own delicious dinner, thus leaving you to your own devices to enjoy an uninterrupted meal.

Ingredients

  • Your choice of dog kibble
  • Paw Lickin' Chickn' Chicken stew
  • Any brand of quality fish oil supplement

Steps

  1. Place the size appropriate amount of kibble into your dog's food bowl.
  2. Mix in half a can of Paw Lickin' Chickn' into the kibble.
  3. Top off with the appropriate portion of fish oil for your dog's weight.
  4. Thats it! Enjoy having a dog that is both full, and happy to recieve such a gift.
